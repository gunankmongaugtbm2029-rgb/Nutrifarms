About NutriFarms

Our Story

NutriFarms was born to solve a simple but urgent problem: People don’t know which food they can trust. With massive adulteration in honey, jaggery, ghee, and oils, families are confused and worried about what they consume every day. We decided to change that — with full transparency, origin identity, and real purity proof.

Our Mission

To bring India pure, accessible, and honest food directly from trusted farms.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted food brand — not by claiming purity, but by proving it.

What Makes Us Different

  • Origin-specific sourcing
  • Farmer partnerships
  • Lab verification
  • QR transparency
  • Honest pricing
